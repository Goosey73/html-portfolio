My top 3 Studio Ghibli Movies

Princess Mononoke

This movie was my first one and I love it. It shows humans' nature as it struggles with nature itself.

Porco Rosso

A man cursed with being a pig struggles through daily life and love.

The Boy and the Herron

A depiction of Miyazaki's life and the meaning of death.
